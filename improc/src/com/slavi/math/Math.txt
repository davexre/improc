Trigonometry
============

sin(a-b) = sin(a)*cos(b) - cos(a)*sin(b)
cos(a-b) = cos(a)*cos(b) + sin(a)*sin(b)
tan(a-b) = (tan(a)-tan(b)) / (1+tan(a)*tan(b))

sin(a+b) = sin(a)*cos(b) + cos(a)*sin(b)
cos(a+b) = cos(a)*cos(b) - sin(a)*sin(b)
tan(a+b) = (tan(a)+tan(b)) / (1-tan(a)*tan(b))

sin(-a)  = -sin(a)
cos(-a)  = cos(a)

sin(90-a)= cos(a)
cos(90-a)= sin(a) 

Derivatives
===========
(sinX)'		= cosX
(cosX)'		= -sinX
(sqrt(X))'	= 1 / sqrt(X)
